## On-Page SEO Checklist

### 1. Title Tags (60 characters max)
```
Home: Mental Health Awareness | 24/7 Free Helpline & Support
About: About Us - Mental Health Awareness Organization
Services: Mental Health Services | Counseling & Therapy Support
Resources: Free Mental Health Resources & Self-Help Guides
Blog: Mental Health Blog | Tips, Guides & Awareness Articles
Get Help: Get Help Now | Free 24/7 Mental Health Helpline
Contact: Contact Us - Mental Health Awareness Support
Donate: Donate to Mental Health Awareness | Support Our Cause
```

### 2. Meta Descriptions (155 characters max)
```
Home: Get immediate, confidential mental health support. Free 24/7 helpline, licensed counselors, and resources. No judgment, just care when you need it most.

Blog: Expert mental health articles on anxiety, depression, stress management, and emotional wellness. Evidence-based tips and support resources.

Get Help: Need help now? Connect with trained counselors 24/7. Confidential, free mental health support via phone, chat, or text. You're not alone.
```

### 3. Header Structure (H1-H6)
```
H1: One per page (main keyword)
H2: Section headings (secondary keywords)
H3: Subsections (related keywords)
```

### 4. Image Optimization
- Alt text with keywords
- Descriptive file names (mental-health-support.jpg)
- Compress images (WebP format)
- Add lazy loading

## Content Strategy

### Blog Post Calendar (Monthly)

| Week | Topic | Long-Tail Keyword |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| 1 | Anxiety Management | "natural ways to calm anxiety attacks at home" |
| 2 | Depression Awareness | "how to help a friend with depression without being pushy" |
| 3 | Stress Relief | "quick stress relief techniques for office workers" |
| 4 | Mental Wellness | "daily habits for better mental health and happiness" |

### Content Guidelines
1. **Word Count**: 1,500-2,500 words per blog post
2. **Keyword Density**: 1-2% (natural placement)
3. **Internal Links**: 3-5 per article
4. **External Links**: 2-3 authoritative sources
5. **Images**: 3-5 relevant images with alt text
6. **Headings**: Use H2/H3 with keywords
7. **Call to Action**: Every post ends with helpline/resources
